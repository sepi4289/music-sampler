Blink 182 is an American rock band create in Poway, California in 1992. When I start listening to this band it was formed by Mark Hoppus, Travis baker and Tom DeLong. In 2015 Delong left the band and Matt Scckiba joing the band. In music videos Blink 182 keeps it entertaining by not taking anything serious. They are well know for making fun at themselves, sarcastic situations, movies, actors, pop artist and pop bands. Most of their songs and videos are intending for adolescents or younger generation. Blink 182 lyrics relates to the struggles and feelings that adolescents go while growing up. Its music focusing on rebellion, girls, dates, divorce, parents,love. This band is a combination of great lyrics, energetic melody, and outstanding live performance.
